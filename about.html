Short Introduction

Hello World, Hope You are having a nice day...I'm Azan Ali, and some of you may know me as Captain K2, my age is 17 years(18 in April). I live in Pakistan with my parents, I like to do Pencil or black and white sketching, I hope you liked my Arts and Thanks for visting my site.

Art Journey

Year 2016, I was 8 years old, one day I was playing some indoor games, and I saw my father coming with a box full of notebooks, drawingbooks, pencils, pens, and colors. this was first time when I was seeing some stuff. which can be a lot of helpful..of course enjoying for me i took a notebook and started to draw dolls. that was like so beginner style people. but as I was so excited i ignored how bad I'm at drawing and that was the evening which made me today's Artist. day by day I draw and filled the notebook, I also drew some Dragons, Buildings and Tech related stuff. in two years my practice made me able to draw poster for my class and for 2 years I decorated my classrooms with posters. Years spent and I achieved the heights of Artistry. finally in 2023 I started to create a notebook once another time and I drew some Rubics Cubes, Minecraft Characters and many more kind of drawings and as always I lost my second notebook so fortunate ufff..The time payback in 2024 my friend drew a South Indian Actor(Rocking Star Yash), and started to bright up in the friendzone not bearable now...I didn't went to school and started to draw a famous Scenrio of Marvel Studios, The last Words of Iron Man I spent 3+ hours to complete it and that day my eraser almost turned black from white due to erasing. Hands tired to hold pencil but the Ego doesn't let me give up and at the last I made what I wanted. and this moment made me sure that Artistry is what who needs patience not quick result and that day I declared myself as an Artist.
